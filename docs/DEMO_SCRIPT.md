# Demo Script

Use this script to demonstrate the local/preprod product without manual explanations.

1. Open the frontend.
2. Show the active tenant, role cards, system-status card and demo seed card.
3. Use global search to find an existing company by name or tax ID.
4. Search for an existing company in the company panel.
5. Clear the search and create a new company.
6. Open the company detail and update one basic field.
7. Review the company summary, chart, outstanding amount and internal notifications.
8. Complete fiscal settings, choose a PDF template and show the active invoice series.
9. Create a customer recipient and filter the customer list.
10. Upload a document in the company documents section and filter by hash or title.
11. Create an invoice draft with discount/withholding fields.
12. Edit one line and confirm totals update.
13. Issue the invoice and confirm fiscal number assignment plus disabled edit.
14. Register a manual payment and filter payments.
15. Download the PDF and show the captured hash.
16. Generate local e-invoice evidence and show LOCAL STUB plus artifact/hash.
17. Register local SIF evidence and show LOCAL STUB plus artifact/hash.
18. Review invoice and company timelines.
19. Export CSV for invoices/customers/audit if needed.
20. Generate a local evidence ZIP.
21. Switch to another tenant and confirm the created company does not appear.

Expected message: this is a fiscal SaaS preproduction flow. It demonstrates tenant isolation, company-centered operations, document storage, customer recipients, invoice lifecycle, fiscal numbering, PDF, payments, local e-invoice, local SIF and evidence export. It does not claim production legal compliance.
