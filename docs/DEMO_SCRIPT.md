# Demo Script

Use this script to demonstrate the local/preprod product without manual explanations.

1. Open the frontend.
2. Show the active tenant and status strip.
3. Search for an existing company by name or tax ID.
4. Clear the search and create a new company.
5. Open the company detail and update one basic field.
6. Complete fiscal settings and show the active invoice series.
7. Create a customer recipient.
8. Upload a document in the company documents section.
9. Create an invoice draft with discount/withholding fields.
10. Edit one line and confirm totals update.
11. Issue the invoice and confirm fiscal number assignment plus disabled edit.
12. Register a manual payment.
13. Download the PDF.
14. Generate local e-invoice evidence.
15. Register local SIF evidence.
16. Generate a local evidence ZIP.
17. Switch to another tenant and confirm the created company does not appear.

Expected message: this is a fiscal SaaS preproduction flow. It demonstrates tenant isolation, company-centered operations, document storage, customer recipients, invoice lifecycle, fiscal numbering, PDF, payments, local e-invoice, local SIF and evidence export. It does not claim production legal compliance.
